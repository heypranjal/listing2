About the Project

Tanya Awas

Tanya Awas (also known as Tanyavas) is a ready-to-move residential plot and independent housing project by Tanyavas Developers Pvt Ltd — a trusted name in Lucknow's residential development landscape.

Located on the highly sought-after Deva Road / Ayodhya–Faizabad Highway corridor, the gated township (available as Tanyavas Phase 7 and Phase 11) sits just 100–150 metres from the Ayodhya-Faizabad Highway, right near Shalimar Paradise — one of Lucknow's most recognisable landmarks on this stretch.

What sets Tanya Awas apart is its ready infrastructure: developed internal roads, sewer systems, and complete drainage networks are already in place, meaning buyers can plan construction or move in immediately without waiting on civic development.

The project falls within the high-tech society range, offering premium plot and housing options at competitive rates between ₹3,000 and ₹3,500 per sq. ft. — ideal for families and investors seeking an established, well-connected residential address in Lucknow.
