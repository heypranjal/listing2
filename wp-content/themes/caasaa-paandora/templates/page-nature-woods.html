About the Project

Nature Woods

Crown Town Phase – II is a thoughtfully planned residential and plotted development by Lucknow Fields Empire Builders & Developers in association with Hitashie Construction & Developer — two trusted names in Lucknow's real estate landscape.

Designed for families who value space, security, and long-term appreciation, Crown Town Phase – II offers an integrated community living experience with well-laid roads, landscaped parks, and essential infrastructure already in place.

With a competitive Basic Sale Price of ₹3,999 per Sq. Ft. and a flexible Purchase + Construction plan, this project is structured for both end-users and discerning investors looking for solid returns in Lucknow's growing residential corridor.
