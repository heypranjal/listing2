About the Developer & Project

SMJ Suraksha Enclave

SMJ Group stands for legacy, precision, and true excellence. With over 16 years of delivering benchmark quality across diverse real estate and civil infrastructure, our journey has been shaped by craftsmanship, commitment, and a belief in doing things right — Always.

From powering marquee developments behind the scenes to now curating our own signature offerings, SMJ is evolving with a singular vision: to craft design-first, experience-led spaces that speak of timeless elegance and structural integrity.

We are not in the business of volume. We build for value, meaning, and permanence. Every creation is a blend of architectural purity, material finesse, and functional grace — designed to become icons of modern living.

Suraksha Enclave is an LDA and RERA-approved plotted township on Purvanchal Road, Lucknow — offering premium European-architecture-inspired plots from 909 to 2002 sq.ft. across 25 acres of lush green development, complete with a Club & Community Center, commercial zones, and a school. Construction is offered separately as a value-added turnkey solution.
