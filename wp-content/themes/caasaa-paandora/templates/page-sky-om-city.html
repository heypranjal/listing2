Vision & About

Sky Om City

Sky Om City isn't just a real estate project — it's a blueprint for the future. Spanning 50 acres, with nearly half the area devoted to lush greenery and open spaces, it's a modern township crafted for those who value well-being, innovation, and thoughtful living.

This is a place where nature breathes life into every corner, and contemporary design harmonises with mindful planning. Sky Om City blends cutting-edge infrastructure with sustainable practices to create not only a place to live but a place to thrive.

With world-class amenities, sustainable practices, and future-ready infrastructure, Sky Om City delivers not just comfort but also long-term value. At its core lies a strong promise — to deliver the highest quality in the shortest possible time. Every square foot reflects precision, innovation, and care.

We're building more than just homes — we're building a community. Where You Don't Just Live, You Lead.

Why Mohanlalganj

Advantages of a Strategic Location

Mohanlalganj is Lucknow's fastest-emerging real estate corridor — backed by government investment, strong infrastructure, and unmatched connectivity.

Prime Location

Situated on State Highway 173, planned to be widened to 220 feet — boosting connectivity, property value, and future accessibility.

Superior Road Connectivity

Well-connected via Kanpur Road, Raebareli Road, Outer Ring Road, and the proposed Ring Road. Close to Lucknow International Airport and Lucknow–Agra Expressway.

Healthcare Access

Located close to well-established hospitals and healthcare centers like SPS and Medanta — providing essential services and added security.

Educational Hub

Situated in a thriving educational zone with strong demand, foot traffic, and potential for student-focused businesses and services.

High Investment Potential

Mohanlalganj is a fast-emerging real estate hotspot with strong connectivity, infrastructure growth, and growing institutional presence.

Infrastructure Growth

Backed by major government road expansion and infrastructure projects, signaling strong long-term development for the region.
